Overview

For this project we took a seasons worth or shots (from the 2016 Chicago Bulls). We want to use this data to show Basketball players where shots fall from. We feel that by providing a seasons worth of data, from a professional team we may educate players of all skill levels where to shoot from.

See our method

Our Libraries

Pandas

We used Pandas to aggregate data and plot the shots from the top 6 players in 2016

Scikit-Learn

We used Gradient Boosting Tress to predict the shots are taken in different positions

Leaflet

We used Leaflet to visualize the shots taken on the court.

Our Result

Our machine learning findings were statistically significant. With a 20% chance of choosing the position correctly initially (assuming a 1/5 probability). Our model was able to improve the probability to 61.7% accuracy (training score). This states our model is predicting the correct position 61.7% of the time, based off the top players like below:

Jimmy Butler
Dwyane Wade
Robin Lopez
Nikola Mirotic
